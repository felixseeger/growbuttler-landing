### Decision Summary

### German Cannabis Market Research (Key Data)
- **Legal Status:** Home cultivation of up to 3 plants per person legalized April 1, 2024
- **Adoption:** 10% of German adults (1.5M+) already cultivating; 11% more interested
- **Market Size:** German cannabis market €2.4B (2025), growing to €9.7B by 2033 (18.9% CAGR)
- **Grower Spending:** €100-500/year per grower on optimization, seeds, equipment
- **Expert Demand:** Master growers command €30-150/hour for consultations
- **No local competitor:** No established German platform combining grow tracking + gig marketplace + trust badges

### GrowButtler Product Architecture
**Three Core Pillars:**
1. **Grow Tracker** (like FocusBlocks): Intelligent calendar for 4 grow phases (2-3w seedling → 4-8w veg → 8-12w flower → 2-4w harvest/cure)
2. **Expert Marketplace** (like LocalJobs): Browse/book local cannabis consultants (Master Grower, Pest Control, Nutrient Expert, Breeding)
3. **Gamified Trust Badges** (like SkillMatch): Workers earn badges (Trusted, Expert, Master, Specialist certifications) + leaderboard + income multipliers

**Revenue Streams:**
- Premium app subscription: €4.99/month (unlimited grow tracking, AI summaries)
- Marketplace commission: 20% on expert gigs (avg €60/hour consultation = €12 platform revenue)
- Labor gigs: 15% commission (trimming, setup, pest management)
- Optional: Premium creator tier (educators selling grow guides)

### 6-Month Revenue Projection (German Market)
- **Month 1:** €100-300 (validation phase, 200-500 free users)
- **Month 2:** €500-1.2k (2-3k growers, first 50 expert gigs)
- **Month 3:** €2-3k (5k growers, 200+ expert marketplace hits)
- **Month 4:** €4-6k (marketplace scaling, 300+ active gigs/mo)
- **Month 5:** €7-10k (network effects accelerating)
- **Month 6:** €12-18k ✅ (exceeds €5k target significantly)

**Break-even:** ~250 paying premium users OR 30-40 gigs/month = profitable by Month 2
